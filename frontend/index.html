Baudet du Poitou

The Baudet du Poitou, also called the Poitevin or Poitou donkey, is a French breed of donkey. It is one of the largest breeds, and jacks (donkey stallions).

Miniature Mediterranean

Miniature Mediterranean Donkeys are a separate breed of donkey originating from the islands of Sardinia and Sicily.

American Mammoth Jackstock

The American Mammoth Jackstock is a breed of North American donkey, descended from large donkeys imported to the United States from about 1785.

ABOUT US

We Have Over 15 Years' Of Experience In Donkey Farm

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm, is a farm that specializes in donkey farming. We have 40 Hectares of land for Pasturing or rather planting grass. We also have a world class standard Slaughter House with a mini ranch built there. Our purpose is beyond providing only employemt to the community but extended to providing a stable agro-economy through donkey farming and that is what ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm is about in partnership with NAPRI Zaria.

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm has also engaged in the breeding of good quality donkeys to produce highly productive and suitable donkeys for enhancement of overall performance in the subsequent generations and to augment production and profitability. With over 15 to 20 years experience in donkey farming, ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm over the past years have also engaged in the production of donkey products such as milk, skin and meat, which have been of great benefit to both the nation and foreign countries at large. Animal Breeders and Skin Guild Association of Nigeria have also proposed to invest in our farm for breeding of more donkeys in Nigeria.
