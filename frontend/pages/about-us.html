Our History

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm is a donkey breeding project in partnership with Nigerian Animal Production Research Institution Zaria. With over 15 to 20 years experience in donkey farming. ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm has also engaged in the breeding of good quality donkeys to produce highly productive and suitable donkeys for enhancement of overall performance in the subsequent generations and to augment production and profitability. ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm over the past years have also engaged in the production of donkey products such as milk, skin and meat, which have been of great benefit to both the nation and foreign countries at large. Animal Breeders and Skin Guild Association of Nigeria have also proposed to invest in our farm for breeding of more Donkey in Nigeria.
