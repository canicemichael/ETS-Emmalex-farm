ABOUT US

WE HAVE 20 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE IN DONKEY FARM

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm, is a farm that specializes in donkey farming. We have 40 Hectares of land for Pasturing or rather planting grass. We also have a world class standard Slaughter House with a mini ranch built there. Our purpose is to provide employemt to the community and provide a sustainable economic system through donkey farming.

  • We have partnership with Nigerian Animal Production Research Institute.
  • Animal Breeders and Skin Guild Association of Nigeria.
  • We are aiming at scaling up to the minimum of two million donkeys before the next 10years.

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm has also engaged in the breeding of good quality donkeys to produce highly productive and suitable donkeys for enhancement of overall performance in the subsequent generations and to augment production and profitability.
